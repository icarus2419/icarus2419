## Full-Stack Developer Focused on Real, Shipped Products

I am a **BSc Computer Science** graduate from the **University of Liverpool** and a software engineer who likes turning ideas into fast, usable, polished web products.

My strongest work is at the intersection of **React**, **JavaScript**, **responsive UI**, **Three.js/WebGL**, and practical full-stack architecture. I have built business websites, interactive portfolios, a multiplayer poker platform, lead-generation tooling, and secure document workflows.

I care about software that feels finished: clear UX, maintainable code, performance, SEO foundations, and the small details that make people trust a product.
